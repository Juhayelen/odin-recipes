Omlette with Kiebasa & Peppers

The completed omlette featuring kielbasa and green peppers.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 Eggs
  • 1 Serving of Kielbasa Sasuage
  • 1-2 Red/Yellow/Green Peppers
  • 1 tsp. Butter
  • Shredded Cheese of your choosing
  • Salt
  • Pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prep your kieblasa and peppers by chopping them up into desired sizes. Larger pieces make it harder to fold the omlette.
  2. Crack your 3-4 eggs into a bowl, try to prevent shells from entering.
  3. Add salt and pepper if desired.
  4. Before whisking, heat pan to medium-low heat and add butter.
  5. While the butter melts, whisk the eggs until homogenized and consistent.
  6. Once eggs are whisked and butter has melted, add the eggs to the pan evenly.
  7. As the edges cook, gently push them slightly inwards and tilt the pan accordingly to move uncooked egg to edges.
  8. Once about half of the bottom of the omlette has solidified, add your shredded cheese. This will help the remaining additions stick.
  9. With the top beginning to solidify, add your chopped kielbasa and peppers.
  10. Flip half of the omlette over and let it cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  11. Plate and serve.
