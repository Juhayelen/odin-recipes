Baked Tilapia: affordable, juicy, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Thawed Tilapia Filets
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Sliced Lemons
  • Gallon Zip-loc bag
  • Oven safe container
  • Oven

Steps:

  1. Place your tilapia filets in a gallon sized zip-loc bag. If unthawed, leave the filled bag in a bowl of water for an hour or in a refridgerator overnight.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and other wanted seasonings into the bag with the tilapia filets.
  3. Making sure the bag is sealed, gently move the bag around to evenly distribute the seasonings across all filets.
  4. Once seasoned, place the filets onto an oven safe container with a small amount of water at the bottom, just enough to cover the bottom.
  5. Add a slice of lemon onto each filet
  6. Set oven to 400F and add container when pre-heated
  7. Let the fish cook for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Check on fish, it should flake when stabbed by fork, and white/brown in color.
  9. If not: cook for 5 more minutes. If cooked: serve and enjoy!
